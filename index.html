TECHNOLOGY DESCRIPTIONS

AMBIENT INTELLIGENCE

Electronic environments that are sensitive and responsive to the presence of people. Ambient intelligence would allow devices in our environments to support people in carrying out their everyday life activities and tasks, using information and intelligence that is hidden in the network connecting these devices.

BEAM-POWERED PROPULSION

Energy that vehicles need to move is beamed to them. This means they do not need to carry that energy, making them lighter and faster. This has potential for space craft to go further into space.

DNA DATA STORAGE

DNA can accurately store massive amounts of data at a density far exceeding that of electronic devices. All the world’s current storage needs for a year could be well met by a cube of DNA measuring about one meter on a side.

GESTURE RECOGNITION

Gesture recognition can be seen as a way for computers to begin to understand human body language. It is the interpretation of the motion of a human user by a computing or electronic device.

HUMAN AUGMENTATION

A field of research that aims to enhance human abilities through medicine or technology. These can enhance physical or mental capabilities.

SWARM ROBOTICS

The ability of robots to collectively explore, communicate and perform complex alterations as a group.
